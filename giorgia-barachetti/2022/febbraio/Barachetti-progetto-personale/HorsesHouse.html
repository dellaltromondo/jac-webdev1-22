JUMPING

Jumping saddles are designed for the sport of jumping, where riders navigate a series of obstacles at high speeds. These saddles are designed to allow the rider to maintain a secure position during takeoff and landing, with a forward-cut flap and a flatter seat than other saddles. Jumping saddles also feature padded knee rolls and thigh blocks to help the rider maintain their position while jumping.

TREKKING

Western saddles are designed for the Western riding style, which emphasizes comfort and stability during long hours of riding. These saddles feature a large, deep seat with high cantle and pommel for added stability, and a horn for securing a rope or lasso. Western saddles often have decorative leatherwork and silver accents, and may include features such as extra D-rings for attaching gear.

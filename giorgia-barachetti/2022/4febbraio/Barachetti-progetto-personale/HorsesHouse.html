JUMPING

Jumping saddles are designed for the sport of jumping, where riders navigate a series of obstacles at high speeds. These saddles are designed to allow the rider to maintain a secure position during takeoff and landing, with a forward-cut flap and a flatter seat than other saddles. Jumping saddles also feature padded knee rolls and thigh blocks to help the rider maintain their position while jumping.

TREKKING

Western saddles are designed for the Western riding style, which emphasizes comfort and stability during long hours of riding. These saddles feature a large, deep seat with high cantle and pommel for added stability, and a horn for securing a rope or lasso. Western saddles often have decorative leatherwork and silver accents, and may include features such as extra D-rings for attaching gear.

Welcome to our Saddles Shop website!

Welcome to our online saddle shop! The first italian saddle shop that uses Italian's products and sell all around the world.

We're a family-owned business that's been making high-quality saddles for over 50 years.Our story began when our founder, John Smith, started crafting saddles by hand in his small workshop. He had a passion for horses and wanted to create saddles that were not only comfortable for riders, but also provided the best support for their equine partners.

Over time, our reputation for making durable and comfortable saddles grew, and we expanded our business to include a larger workshop and more skilled craftspeople. Today, we have a team of expert saddle makers who use the latest technology and traditional techniques to create saddles that meet the needs of riders of all levels and disciplines.

We take pride in the fact that our saddles are made in the USA and are built to last. Each saddle is crafted with precision and care, using only the finest materials. We work closely with our customers to ensure that their saddles fit perfectly and provide maximum comfort for both rider and horse.

In addition to saddles, we also offer a range of accessories, such as stirrups, reins, and saddle pads. We're committed to providing our customers with everything they need to enjoy their riding experience to the fullest.

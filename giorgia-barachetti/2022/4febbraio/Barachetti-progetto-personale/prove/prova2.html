Welcome to our online saddle shop! The first italian saddle shop that uses Italian's products and sell all around the world.

We're a family-owned business that's been making high-quality saddles for over 50 years. Our story began when our founder, John Smith, started crafting saddles by hand in his small workshop. He had a passion for horses and wanted to create saddles that were not only comfortable for riders, but also provided the best support for their equine partners.
